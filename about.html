Who We Are

Welcome to UCY Consultancy

UCY Consultancy Limited was established in London in 2018. UCY Consultancy is giving consultancy services in Marketing, Advertising as well as Supply Chain & Logistics which became the most important sector after Covid-19 crisis. Our core mission is to continue to provide clients with great service and innovation from a trusted leader in specialist Marketing, Advertising, Logistics & Supply Chain Consulting. Our Marketing and Advertising consultancy service aims that to increase your company's brand awareness and improve your company's sales via social media marketing activities. On the other hand, our supply chain & logistics services aims that to reduce the costs of supply chain.
